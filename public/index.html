About Metro Fitness App

Metro Fitness is a fully functional application dealing with nutrition, diet and physical wellness. The thing that makes us different is that we are walking our users through the entire process! Tracking calories can be tedious in the beginning for people with sporadic eating habits, considering that one couldn't possibly know how much fat, carbs, or protein they should intake in a day to see the results they want. Luckily we have an algorithm in place that calculates the recommended maintenance calories that is specifically tailored to the user's body.
